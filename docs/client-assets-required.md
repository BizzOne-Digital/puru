## Temporary vs required
- **Temporary stock photography:** licensed Unsplash industrial/application scenes stored locally with provenance in `docs/asset-sources.md`.
- **Still required from client:** branded Safe Solution® / CRS™ / Clean Step™ photos, packaging (if publicly approved), brochures, and any certification marks the company is authorized to display.

Do not add fake bottles, labels, packaging, certification marks, reports, or testimonials. Use only client-approved assets or the documented temporary stock photography / abstract illustrations.
