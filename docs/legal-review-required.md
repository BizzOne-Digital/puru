# Legal Review Required

The Privacy Policy and Terms of Use were expanded into production-ready business website wording using only verified public company details.

Qualified legal review is still required before final approval, especially for:

- Privacy obligations by jurisdiction
- Cross-border processing wording
- Data retention periods
- Service-provider disclosures
- Cookie and analytics disclosures if analytics are enabled
- Limitation of liability and indemnity wording
- Governing law wording
- Product, surface, insurance, and investment disclaimers
- Trademark attribution wording
